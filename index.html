Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our property management services

What percentage do you charge for property management?

We charge 5-10% of the monthly rent, which is covered through rent increases. This means no upfront costs for you, and our fee scales with your rental income.

How do you find quality tenants?

We use a comprehensive 7-point screening process including background checks, income verification, employment history, and reference checks to ensure only reliable tenants.

What happens if a tenant doesn't pay rent?

We handle all rent collection and follow-up procedures. Our 100% rent collection rate is backed by our thorough screening process and proactive tenant management.

How often do you inspect the property?

We conduct detailed property inspections every two months to identify and address any issues early, protecting your investment and keeping tenants satisfied.

Can I terminate the agreement early?

Yes, our agreement is flexible. After the initial 11-month period, you can terminate with 30 days notice if you're not satisfied with our services.
